Copycat Disneyland Grilled Cheese

Description

For this copycat Disneyland grilled cheese, provolone and Cheddar slices encase a Cheddar and cream cheese spread in hearty white bread. A garlic-mayonnaise smear on the outside is the secret to grilled perfection.

Ingrediants

  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 2/3 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt, divided
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely minced
  • 8 slices hearty white bread
  • 8 slices sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 8 slices provolone cheese

Steps

  1. Stir cream cheese, shredded cheese, heavy cream, and 1/4 teaspoon salt together in a small bowl until well combined; set aside.
  2. Stir mayonnaise, garlic, and remaining salt together in a separate bowl; set aside.
  3. Place 2 slices Cheddar cheese on each of 4 pieces of bread. Spread cream cheese mixture evenly among the 4 Cheddar-topped slices; top each with 2 slices provolone cheese. Add remaining bread slices on top.
  4. Spread half of mayonnaise mixture evenly over tops of sandwiches.
  5. Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Place sandwiches in skillet, mayonnaise side down. Spread the top of each sandwich with remaining mayonnaise mixture.
  6. Cook sandwiches until golden brown on both sides, flipping occasionally until cheese is melted and bread is well toasted.
